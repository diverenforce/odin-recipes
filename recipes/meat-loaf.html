Meat loaf

Description

I chose meat loaf for my first recipe as display because I have never eaten it in my life and I definitely would like to eat it some day. This is one of those food that I have heard but never eaten. I heard it from a show called Adventure Time and it is the main character Finn's favorite food. The ice king revealed this information in episode 7 of season 3.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 slices bread, crumbled
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup shredded carrot
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
